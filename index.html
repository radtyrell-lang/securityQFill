FAQ

How accurate is it?

Accuracy depends on how well your policy documents cover the questions asked. Rows with a strong policy match are high-confidence. Rows where no relevant policy content was found are flagged for manual review rather than guessed.

What if AI doesn’t know the answer?

Those rows are flagged with a “low confidence” label and left for you to fill manually. SecurityQFill never invents an answer where it doesn’t have policy backing.

Does it support Excel?

Yes. Upload CSV or XLSX questionnaires and download your filled file in the same format.

Can I edit answers before downloading?

Not in v1 — the filled file downloads directly. You can edit it in Excel or Google Sheets after download.

Is my API key stored?

No. Your key is held in your browser for the duration of the session only. It is never sent to or stored on our servers.

Which AI providers are supported?

Anthropic (Claude), OpenAI (GPT-4o), Azure OpenAI, and Google Vertex AI. If you have an existing enterprise LLM contract with any of these, you can use it.

What questionnaire formats are supported?

PDF, DOCX, CSV, and XLSX. The most common formats: CAIQ, SIG, Microsoft vendor security forms, and freeform DOCX tables.

Is there a Chrome extension?

Yes. SecurityQFill will be available as both a web app and a Chrome extension. The extension adds one extra capability: if your questionnaire is a web form, you can fill it in-place directly from your browser without downloading or re-uploading anything.

Can I retry if something goes wrong?

Yes. If processing fails for any reason, you get a full refund. No questions asked.
